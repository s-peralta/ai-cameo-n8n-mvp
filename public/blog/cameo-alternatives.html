Why People Are Looking for Cameo Alternatives

Cameo remains the most well-known personalized video platform, but it has significant drawbacks that drive users to look elsewhere:

High prices. The most popular celebrities on Cameo charge $100-$500 or more per video. Even lesser-known creators start at $25. For a fun birthday surprise, these prices can be hard to justify.

Slow delivery. Cameo videos are recorded at the celebrity's convenience, which means you might wait 1 to 7 days. Some requests expire entirely if the celebrity doesn't respond. This makes Cameo unreliable for last-minute gifts.

Inconsistent quality. Some celebrities read your instructions carefully and deliver a thoughtful, personal message. Others give a 15-second generic shoutout. You don't know what you'll get until the video arrives.

Request declines. Celebrities can decline requests at any time, leaving you without a gift after waiting days.
